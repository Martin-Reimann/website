About

Thanks for checking in. I am an associate professor of marketing at the University of Arizona with affiliations in cognitive science, psychology, and veterinary medicine.

My research broadly deals with the way consumers strive for desirable things and people, how they feel about these things and people, and whether their actions will lead to longer-term outcomes such the product purchases or a new relationship.

Important to me is to study consumers through the lens of different methods, including asking them about their opinions, attitudes, and feelings, measuring their neurophysiological responses, and observing their behavior.
